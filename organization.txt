##Status effects
t-&#-<status>

(Replace t with team. Ex: strata-a0-rally)
really doesn't matter too much cause it's not like status effects will break often


## Items/Liquids

Replace & with the letter
&#-#&-<name>

### Insertion rules

Adding a new item, change number on left first, then letter
Inserting a new item, change letter on right

If letter doesn't work, use the closest halfway point in the number
(Ex if stuck between a 2 and an 8, use a 5)

---------------------------
a0-0a-debris
 <- a0-b0-example
a1-a0-electrum
---------------------------


---------------------------
a0-0a-debris
 <- a0-a5-example
a0-b0-carbolith
---------------------------


## Units

Replace & with the letter, team goes in the %
%-&#-#&-<name>


### Insertion rules
same as items, just ignore the first team bit
